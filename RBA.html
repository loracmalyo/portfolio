Rights and Brands Asia

Company: Rightd and Brands Asia, IP Licensing

Position: Creative Brand Manager, APAC

Date: Sep 24 - Present

Services: Creative direction, marketing and branding strategy, design approvals

‍

Company

Rights and Brands Asia is an IP licensing agency, holding the licensing rights of brands including Moomin, TOMICA, Opanchu Usagi, Sho-chan and more, in the East Asia and South East Asia market. The company is a joint venture between ITOCHU Coorporatin, PPW Sports and Entertainment (HK) Ltd. and Moomin Characters Oy Ltd.


Problem

The company managed four distinct Japanese lifestyle and character IPs, each with fragmented visual identities across digital and physical touchpoints. Brand consistency was lacking, approval processes were slow, and external designers often delivered assets that didn’t align with brand strategy or licensor expectations. This resulted in delayed launches, diluted brand perception, and inefficient cross-team collaboration.


Process

Brand Guideline Development & Enforcement

I created comprehensive brand guidelines for each IP, covering logo usage, color palettes, typography, and asset specifications for both digital and print.

Cross-Platform Asset Production

Re-designed and art-directed a wide range of materials—including UI designs, packaging mock-ups, social media content, event POSM, and promotional videos—ensuring visual coherence across all consumer touchpoints.

Streamlined Creative Workflow

Provide on-boarding design training for each licensee. Implemented a structured feedback and approval process using mood boards, annotated markups, and weekly syncs with licensors and external designers to ensure alignment from concept to delivery.

Outcome

Improved Brand Consistency

Achieved 95%+ adherence to brand guidelines across 1000+ monthly assets.

Scoail Following Boost

Boosted social media engagement by over 300% on key campaigns through cohesive, platform-optimized content.

Enhanced Stakeholder Satisfaction

Increased licensor satisfaction scores through transparent collaboration and strategic creative presentations.

Stronger Visual Impact

Produced and executed designs for exhibitions and events, created motion graphics for all brands including GIF emojis, videos, animations and more.
